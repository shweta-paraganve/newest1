 FAQ

What Is Hack Club?

Hack Club is student's coding and developing club. Chat, meet new friends, code together, share your work.

What is Hack Club COMPESA?

Hack Club COMPESA is student coding club in SITCOE. Every member in hack Club will work as Project Leaders. They will be work on open-source projects. They also invited to join our Slack community.

How to Join Hack Club COMPESA?

You can apply for Co-Lead position or you can also join as member by filling form and applied students will undergo with small interview process. Our recruiting team will be select the shortlisted students.

Can anyone become the Project Leader For Hack Club ?

Yes, Anyone can apply for this post. Applicants will be going through small interview process.

What Skills Required to become Project Leader in Hack Club COMPESA ?

1.Basic Programming Knowledge
2.problem solving and decision making
3.Good Communication
4.Technical Skills

Benefits to join Hack Club COMPESA?

1. you’ll join a private space for Hack Club leaders to ask questions & chat, share projects, & attend weekly live events.
2. From Hack Night to AMAs to weirder events, the Slack community has live events for leaders & members alike every week.
3.Free subscriptions to Figma Pro, repl.it, Postman and more for running a great club.
